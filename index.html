What We Do & Why We Do

What if your household waste could be a money making machine? Yes!! You Heard it Right. With this brand new business model, we aim to turn your Household waste into Gold. We'll collect your garbage from homes, pay you for it, and turn it into a valuable product. We will change the way the world sees garbage. We will sort, collect, transport and sell the collected bags of trash with a live, onsite store.

India is a country in need of massive amounts of waste management. The country's population is about 1.3 billion people with a per capita waste production rate of about 4.5 kg per person. This means that the country produces about 5 million tons of solid waste every day, and only 2% of it gets recycled or composted. The other 98% ends up in landfills where it releases methane into the atmosphere, making our climate change more severe than it would be otherwise by contributing to global warming. This means that India is stuck with a massive environmental crisis that will take decades if not generations to fix India's dump yards and landfills are one of the most pressing problems facing the country today.
